Why Zeitoun
The Science Behind the Oil

Measurable health benefits, not just tradition.

780 mg/kg Polyphenols
Three times the industry average. Preserved by early harvest and cold extraction under 27°C. Oleocanthal and oleacein degrade rapidly with heat — ours don't.
Anti-Inflammatory
Daily consumption reduces C-reactive protein and IL-6. Documented in 30+ years of Mediterranean diet research.
Cardiovascular Health
Oleic acid (75% of our oil) is associated with reduced LDL cholesterol and improved HDL ratio in clinical trials.
Immune Support
Vitamin E, squalene, and natural antioxidants protect cells from oxidative stress, preserved in biologically active form by cold extraction.
Skin and Hair
Squalene and vitamin E deeply hydrate skin and strengthen hair. Used both topically and through diet for measurable improvements.
0.18% Acidity
IOC standard is under 0.8%. Our oil tests at 0.18% — exceptional freshness, correct ripeness, immediate pressing.
